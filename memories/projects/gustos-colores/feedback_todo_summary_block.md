When editing `TODO.md`, the first section must be a short summary block — total open count, where the work clusters, what's blocking, what's not blocking. Update it in the same edit that touches any item below.

**Why:** The user asked for a survey of open work and liked the closing summary I wrote ("Total: 21 open items. Cobrand ops dominates; the heaviest cluster is the Play Console driver hardening (items 5, 8, 9, 11) — each is a real selector/Material-protection fight, none blocking mandala internal testing today."). They told me to bake this into TODO.md and keep it current.

**How to apply:** Whenever you check off, add, or reword a TODO item, recompute the summary in the same turn — count, cluster characterisation, blocking-vs-not. Don't let the summary drift out of sync with the body. Keep it short (2-4 sentences) and load-bearing — say what's the heaviest fight and what's not in the critical path, not just totals.
