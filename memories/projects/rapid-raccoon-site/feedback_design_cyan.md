Neon cyan `#00f2ff` (with supporting `#74f5ff` and `#00dbe7`) is the locked-in signature accent for the Rapid Raccoon site. Used for headlines, CTAs, glow shadows, pills, hairline borders, dot-grid body background, and link underlines.

**Why:** During the initial Stitch reskin on 2026-04-24, the user approved the Mascot palette and volunteered _"i do particularly like the neon cyan ;-D"_ — unprompted, positive taste signal, not a correction.

**How to apply:** When suggesting design tweaks, color changes, or new components on this site, default to preserving or amplifying the neon cyan. Don't propose warmer/earthier accent shifts (orange, amber, magenta) without a specific reason tied to what the user asked for. If a third accent is ever needed, lean toward a cool complement (violet, ice white, deep teal) rather than something that fights the cyan.
